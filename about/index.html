I love to share ideas about programming, whether it is though speaking, producing video tutorials, or engaging in the learning process to deliver the best materials for newcomers.

As a software engineer, my passions are programming language design, and making types work for you.

I currently work at the Scala Center on the Scala 2 and 3 compilers, Scala's documentation, and TASTy Query.
